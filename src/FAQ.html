FAQ

Why is a Video is not loading?

Videos may not load due to poor internet connection. Please ensure you have a strong connection.

Is there a HD version for this video?

All videos are available in HD.

Why is the sound distorted?

Distortion may occur when your audio volume is too high resulting in clipping. Try to reduce the volume of your device.

Why is the video randomly stopping?

Buffering is quite normal when streaming online. This could be because of slow internet.

When I change the quality of a video, nothing happens

After changing the quality of a video you will need to wait for a couple minutes for the higher resolution to take effect.

How do I make the video go full screen?

There is a button available within the viewport that allows you to toggle full screen mode..

What browsers are supported?

All browsers are supported. Just make sure your browser is updated.

How do you handle my privacy?

We take data protection very serious. Kindly visit our privacy page for more information.

How can I contact you?

Visit our contact page for more information.

Why isn't the video starting at the beginning?

Our platform was designed to allow you start playing your videos from where you had previously stopped. You can always start from the beginning if needed.
