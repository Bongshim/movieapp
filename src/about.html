About Us

TIIDEFlix - Best Place for Movies

TIIDEFlix is an innovative Nigerian movie streaming platform that offers both local and foreign movies and TV- shows. At TIIDEFlix, we want to entertain the world. Whatever your taste, and no matter where you live, we give you access to best-in-class TV shows, movies and documentaries. Our members control what they want to watch, when they want it, with no ads, in one simple subscription. In an effort to promote the Nigerian movie industry and bring back the nostalgia for Nigerian home movies, we provide movies in Nigerian languages.
